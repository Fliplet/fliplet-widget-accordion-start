How to use accordions

This heading is one of 2 components you’ll need to form an expanding accordion, or a group of accordions:

  • Accordion Heading (this component)
  • End of Accordion Group

Name your heading and then go back to the app screen.

On your app screen, hit enter after the heading and add all the content for your accordion directly underneath the heading.

Add more heading components as required if you would like a group of accordions.

When you’re ready, add an End of Accordion Group component. Repeat for any further accordions you may want.

For more information and inspiration, read Fliplet's guide to accordions.
